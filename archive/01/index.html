Frequently Asked Questions

If you have questions that are not listed here, send them to us via email.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No. You are on a monthly contract and can cancel any time?

Are there any setup fees?

No. We are currently waiving setup and onboarding fees for new customers.

How many podcasts does each package support?

Each package supports unlimited new episodes per month for a single podcast show.

Can you clip my old episodes?

Yes. Contact us with a link to your podcast and the number of episodes you would like clipped and we will provide a quote with price and turn around time.

Who owns the content BGDY creates from my podcast?

You or your business owns 100% of all content we produce for you.

How many clips will you provide per episode?

We don't limit the number of good clips we pull from an episode. In general, we find 8-10 clips per episodes. If you publish your podcast at least once per week, this gives you a new clip or post to publish every day of the week to consistently engage on social media.

Do you clip video podcasts as well?

Yes, we clip both audio or video podcasts. We also clip Zoom recordings that you share via a Google Drive or Dropbox link.

What format does my podcast need to be provided in?

Send us a link to your podcast on Apple Music, Spotify or YouTube. That's all we need to get started. We'll pull your new episodes and start clipping them as soon as you publish your latest episode.

What upload and scheduling software do you use?

We integrate into your current publishing software. If you aren't using any social scheduling software, we can make a recommendation based on your goals.
