About

Greetings from Seattle.

BGDY Creative is an ambitious global team of creators, problem solvers, and thinkers who help organizations find, engage and convert their next customer with engaging articles, podcasts and short-form videos. We help organizations establish their voice, expand to new platforms and build authentic customer relationships. Prior to BGDY we worked at Amazon, Deloitte Digital, Microsoft, and hyper-growth startups. Four values drive us. Dream big. Stay curious. Be great. Do you. The latter is our most important value. We hold this bar for both ourselves and the organizations we serve. What does it mean to "do you"? Deeply understand yourself and your environment. Don't fake it. And never sacrifice what makes you remarkable. From there it is about sharing your voice across the internet. That's where we come in to amplify your best moments.

Ready to unlock the power of short-form video?

Organic online content is one of the most cost-effective paths to build an audience and generate leads. This is especially true for short-form videos.
